About Me

Short Bio

I didn’t study anything related to computer science at university. I actually studied Mass Communication but along the way, I developed a deep interest in technology and web development. That curiosity led me to learn and also transitioned to frontend development, where I now enjoy building responsive, accessible, and user-friendly web experiences with React, TypeScript, and SCSS.

My HNG Goals

Through the HNG Internship, I aim to sharpen my frontend development skills by working on real-world projects and collaborating with talented developers. I want to improve my problem-solving abilities, learn best practices for teamwork and version control, and gain hands-on experience that prepares me for professional opportunities in tech. My ultimate goal is to grow into a well-rounded engineer who can build scalable, high-quality web applications.
